About FreshBite

FreshBite is an online food ordering platform designed to provide customers with a fast, secure and convenient way to order delicious food from their favourite restaurants. Our mission is to deliver fresh food quickly while ensuring excellent customer service and a seamless ordering experience. This project was developed using HTML, CSS and JavaScript as part of a Web Development Internship Project.
